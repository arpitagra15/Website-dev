About Shakes&Shakes

Our story, mission, and commitment to quality

Our Story

How we became your favorite juice bar

2020

Humble Beginnings

Founded in a small kitchen with just three blender and a dream, Shakes&Shakes started as a passion project to bring healthy, delicious beverages to our community.

2021

First Location

We opened our first storefront in downtown, quickly gaining popularity for our innovative flavor combinations and commitment to fresh ingredients.

2022

Expanding the Menu

Introduced our line of superfood smoothies and cold-pressed juices, catering to health-conscious customers looking for nutrient-packed options.

2023

Community Recognition

Voted "Best Juice Bar" by City Magazine and featured in several health and wellness publications for our innovative approach to healthy beverages.

Our Mission

At Shakes&Shakes, we believe that healthy can be delicious. Our mission is to provide our community with the freshest, most nutritious beverages made from high-quality, natural ingredients.

We're committed to sustainability, sourcing locally whenever possible, and reducing our environmental impact through eco-friendly packaging and practices.
